How it works

From posted to paid

Three steps, whether you're outsourcing a chore or picking up your next gig.

01

Post or browse

Customers describe the task, set a budget and deadline. Workers browse open tasks that match their skills.

02

Apply & get chosen

Workers apply with a short message. The customer reviews applicants and picks the one worker to assign.

03

Complete & rate

Once the job is done, the customer marks it complete and leaves a rating that builds the worker's profile.
